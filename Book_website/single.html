About:

This book on Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel offers a deep dive into the life and legacy of one of India’s most influential leaders. Often referred to as the “Iron Man of India,” Sardar Patel played a pivotal role in unifying the country after its independence and was crucial in shaping modern India’s political landscape.
